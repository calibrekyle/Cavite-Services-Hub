How It Works

Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage. Fill in the required information including your name, email address, and password. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email. Click on the link in the email to verify your account.

To post a service request, log in to your account and click on the 'Add Post' button. Provide the necessary details about the service you need, such as the type of service, description, and any other relevant information. Once done, click 'Submit' to post your request.

To bookmark a post, click on the 'Bookmark' button found on the service post you are interested in. The post will then be saved to your bookmarked list, which you can access from your profile.

To contact a service provider, go to their profile page and click on the 'Send Message' button. You can then send them a message with your inquiry or request. Alternatively, you can contact them through the details provided on their post.

To edit your profile, log in to your account and go to your profile page. Click on the 'Edit Profile' button, make the necessary changes, and then click 'Save' to update your profile information.
